About Us

Gym and Fitness was founded in 2022 as a family owned and operated business specialising in supplying high-quality gym equipment at great prices.

For the past years helping customers reach their fitness goals has been at the heart of what we do and why we do it! We live and breathe our six core values and four brand promises — which speak of our commitment to our customers, staff, the industry and our business as a whole.We know that building a positive culture is incredibly important. We believe in encouraging, supporting, challenging, learning and growing to be the best! Our flexible working solutions, gym rebates and educational opportunities create a positive work/life balance for all our employees.
